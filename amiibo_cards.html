Amiibo Cards

About Cards

Amiibo cards were launched in October 2015, and the first to be released were the 'Animal Crossing' cards for the 3DS. The first cards were announced for use with the new release game, 'Happy Home Designer', and as amiibo became more popular, compatibility with them was integrated into the New 3DS system.

To use amiibo figures, tap the figure to either the external NFC reader, New Nintendo 3DS system, Wii U, or Nintendo Switch, and watch the magic unfold in-game.
