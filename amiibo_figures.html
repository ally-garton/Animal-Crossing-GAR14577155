About Figures

Amiibo figures were launched in November 2014, and the first to be released were the 'Super Smash Bros.' figures for the Wii U. In 2015, the first 'Animal Crossing' amiibos were announced for use with the new release game, 'Happy Home Designer', and as amiibo became more popular, compatibility with them was integrated into the New 3DS system.

To use amiibo figures, tap the figure to either the external NFC reader, New Nintendo 3DS system, Wii U, or Nintendo Switch, and watch the magic unfold in-game.
