Amiibo Cards

Amiibo cards and figures for 'Animal Crossing' were released in 2015 for use initially with 'Happy Home Designer'. As they became more popular, compatibility was added to 3DS systems so no external device was required to use them, and an update was released for 'Animal Crossing: New Leaf' to make all the amiibo compatible with the game.

The Japanese toy company, 'Sanrio', collaborated with Nintendo to create six new characters on amiibo cards which can be used in-game.
